3 Leches

The tres leches cake, tart or cake is a traditional Latin American dessert. It consists of a sponge bathed in three types of milk: evaporated milk, milk cream and condensed milk, which give it its name. It is usually accompanied with an egg white meringue and maraschino cherries and sprinkled with ground cinnamon. Usually the recipe does not call for butter and therefore has a spongy texture.
