Salchipapa

Ingredients

  • 2 units of medium potatoes
  • 3 units of sausages
  • 1 tablespoon mustard
  • 1 dessert spoon of ketchup
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 pinch of pepper
  • 250 milliliters of oil to fry

How to make Cold Tres Leches Dessert:

  1. Wash the potatoes very well, peel them and cut them into short strips. Preheat the oil over medium-high heat in a large saucepan or skillet.
  2. Wash the potato wedges with water and dry them very well with a paper towel or an absorbent cloth. It is important that they are dry when frying.
  3. Throw the potato wedges into the skillet when the oil is very hot. Fry for 5 to 8 minutes or until just beginning to brown, the texture of the potatoes in this dish is more soft than crunchy so don't toast them too much.
  4. Cut the sausages at a steep angle for a more playful look.
  5. Boil the sausages in water for 5 to 10 minutes , when they inflate they will be ready. Strain the water very well so that the potatoes do not get wet when integrating them.
  6. Assemble the potatoes with the sausages and toss around a bit , serve with sauce on top or on the side for a spread. Your salchipapas will be ready to share and enjoy!

  7. Suggestions

    Salchipapas are the favorite dish of adults and children when it comes to sharing time with friends and/or family, it is very common to find them both in bar menus and in street food carts. You can add or remove what you like, the important thing is to do it with potatoes and sausages so as not to lose the concept of the original recipe.
